HOW TO MAKE PANCAKE

Ingredient To Use:

Borrowed a nice picture from google.

  • All purpose flour
  • Milk
  • Egg
  • butter
  • baking powder
  • sugar & salt
  • vanila extract

    • Below are the steps to make it...

    1. Combine the milk,egg,melted butter,sugar and vanilla extract into a large bowl and mix it thorougly
    2. Stir in the all-purpose flour,salt and baking powder
    3. Heat a large buttered frying pan over a medium heat
    4. Add 1/4 cup of the pancake batter to the frying pan
    5. cook for 1 minutes on both sides
    6. serve when the batter has turned golden brown
    7. it can be eaten with either honey or maple syrup
